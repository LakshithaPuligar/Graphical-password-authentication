Graphical Password Authentication

Secure your accounts with intuitive graphical passwords. A smarter way to protect your data.

What is Graphical Password Authentication?

A security system where users use images as passwords instead of traditional text passwords, enhancing security and usability providing a secure alternative.(GPA) is a security method that replaces traditional text-based passwords with images, patterns, or graphical elements. This technique enhances security by reducing vulnerabilities to brute-force and dictionary attacks while improving usability through visual memory. GPA is widely used in banking, mobile security, and enterprise systems, offering protection against keyloggers and phishing attacks..

How It Works

Users select a sequence of images as their password, clicking on specific points in a 3x3 grid.
